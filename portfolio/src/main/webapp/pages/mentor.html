Welcome Mentors!

We appreciate your willingness to help our mentees and encourage them to kickstart their career in the tech industry.


Become a Mentor

We have so many new programmers who are eager to jump into the tech industry and could use your help with finding their way through their journey. We appreciate any help we can get, whether that means helping one mentee or many!

To become a mentor, visit our sign-up page.


Helping your Mentees

Properly supporting your mentee is important in their mentorship experience. To assist with communicating with your mentees, decide how to keep in touch with them, whether that is by giving them your email, using Google Chat, Discord, or whatever you find works best. Your mentees will be eager to ask questions, so make sure you will have enough time to respond to them.
