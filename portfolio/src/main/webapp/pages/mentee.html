Welcome Mentees!

Stepping into the tech industry can be scary and overwhelming, but our mentors are to help! You will be paired with a mentor if you so choose, who will be able to assist you with technical questions, problems, or to give advice about your career.


Connecting with your Mentor

Your mentor will be a valuable resource for helping to boost you into the tech field. Your mentor will decide how you will be communicating, so make sure to take advantage of your connection!


How your Mentor can help

Your mentor is here to help you with anything relating to your tech journey, including coding problems, figuring out where to start, defining your learning path, giving professional advice, or anything else that your heart desires! Nobody is an expert in everything, so be patient with your mentor if they are also unfamiliar with something.
