Mentorship Simplified

Creating a community of support. One student at a time.

Are you ready to make a change?

We are here to help.

Our organization is dedicated to changing the face of the tech industry. We believe that everyone should be provided the resources needed to get ahead. Welcome to our community. We are a group of learners dedicated to making the tech industry a more inclusive place. We believe that mentorship can be used to dramatically transfrom the lives of others. Our platform is a place where industry leaders can connect with individuals that need help navigating the tech industry. We built this platform for individuals from marginalized groups. Especially, those that have been historically excluded from the tech industry. We believe that this inclusive community can help combat some of the diversity issues currently affecting the tech industry. This platform will benefit a wide range of users. Individuals with little experience will be given access to a wealth of resources while also being given the opportunity to network with experienced professionals and industry leaders.
